Ranking my tastes

Here is a general ranking of my music tastes.

  1. Musicals and Precure songs (they're tied for number one)
  2. Various theme songs (I just love listening to a bunch of different theme songs)
  3. Pokemon songs
  4. choral music
  5. Christmas songs
  6. Classic artists like Michael Jackson, Beyonce, sometimes Ariana Grande and the like
  7. Nicki Minaj
  8. songs from other animes (like Teppen E Dash from Inazuma Eleven Ares)
  9. Victorious songs
  10. Lemonade Mouth (particularly Determinate and Breakthrough)
  11. The Fresh Beat Band
  12. songs and covers from the hit musical comedy drama series Glee (it aired on FOX)
  13. songs from movies (motion picture soundtracks)
  14. popular songs (mostly pop)
  15. Disney Channel songs
  16. some YouTuber songs
  17. Some other rap songs (not many)
